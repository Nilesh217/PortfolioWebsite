About Me

Background

Hello! My name is Nilesh Dwivedi, and I am a passionate coder and web developer. I enjoy blending creativity with code to turn my ideas into reality. My enthusiasm for computer science, particularly in coding and development, has been a driving force in my life. I began coding in the 8th grade and have tackled challenges ranging from basic input and output to complex algorithms.

I am currently a first-year B.Tech student from Surya Nagar, Lucknow. I completed my 12th grade at St. Mary's Convent Inter College, R.D.S.O., Lucknow.

My work philosophy centers around building with purpose. Driven by curiosity, I am always eager to learn about the latest technologies and improve my skills. I enjoy experimenting with new ideas and features, as well as exploring reviews and blogs related to my projects.

Outside of coding, I love reading, playing chess, and occasionally playing badminton with my friends.
